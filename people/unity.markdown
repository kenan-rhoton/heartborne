#UNITY

-   Sequence:
-   Player 1 reveals event
-   Players volunteer (or not)

-   If players reach a decision, volunteer 1 sets for the mission
    -   Players may give parting gift
-   Else
    -   Resolve the mission's despair effect
    -   Player 2 reveals event...

-   Player 2 reveals event
-   Players volunteer (or not)

-   If players reach a decision, volunteer 2 sets for the mission
    -   Players may give parting gift
    -   Volunteer 1 resolves his event
        -   First, he resolves success
        -   Then, he resolves any missteps
        -   Finally, he rejoins the other players
        -   Player 3 reveals event...
-   Else
    -   Resolve the mission's despair effect
    -   Player 3 reveals event...
